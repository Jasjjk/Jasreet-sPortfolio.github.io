Blogs by Jasreet-My Imaginary City

Imagination 1

In this city people wake up at the golden hour. It is a city which goes on getting beautiful and green as the perception of people changes. The better the perception the more pretty it appears. There are no alarm clocks. The city relies on the cocks voice and body's natural clock to wake up. It is a city of dream in which people from their boring and caged life want to escape to and pursue their passion. People are very keen to come here and pursue their passion and the simple life where there is freedom. You won't be able to get to know a lot about the city just by looking at the outward beautiful landscape. The city is made up of the beautiful crops growing abundantly because of the farmers hard work, laughter of children playing and lost in their imagination, gossips and the laughing exercise of old people and people who are living in the present. The city keeps on prospering because of these things

Imagination 2

The city is different from the bustling life where people are a slave to work and think that they are happy but inside they are just tired of the rat race. Here we will be able to feel the clear and pollution free air. Its more like a village. People are more caring and helpful. One would be able to feel the balmy air, green hills and rich woods of a village. It is a place where you see everyone in a very raw manner. They are appreciated for being who they are and there are no tags attached to them. It was unlike the city where people were appreciated for for being themselves but for the qualities that possessed that was quite important for being appreciated in the society. In the city, there are seven gates. You have to be careful which gate to enter the city from. Some gates are hume, and there are beautiful carved out designs on it and some are very narrow and dull looking.

Imagination 3

You will get into a maze if you enter the huge and beautiful gate but if you enter the narrow gate you will be able to enjoy the scenery and the nature around you. Here life is different from the huge cities where people are stuck in a never ending cycle of routine. Here there is freedom and people enjoy and live in the present. Here noone has a set routine in their mind and everyone just go with the flow. People are deeply in touch with nature. They spend a lot of time outdoors and enjoy the many gifts of nature and feel grateful everyday. In this city people are happy with what they are doing. When you enter the city from the narrow gate the city is actually beautiful and not just an illusion of being beautiful. People are not deceived when they come to this city as the reality matches their expectations. Even the wild animals are friendly with the humankind and vice versa. It is a city where people believes in God and also work hard. They are grateful each and everyday for their beautiful life.
